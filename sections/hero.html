Can AI Develop a Sense of Self?

Cognitive Science Inspired Evaluations for Embodied MLLMs

Shengyu Guo, Tongrui Ye, Jianbo Zhang, Zicheng Zhang, Chunyi Li, Guangtao Zhai
Shanghai AI Lab

Existing embodied benchmarks focus on external objectives and largely neglect self-centric intelligence. To address this gap, we draw inspiration from cognitive science and translate its influential theories and experimental paradigms into evaluation frameworks for embodied MLLMs.
